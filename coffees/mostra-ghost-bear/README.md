## What to Expect

Ghost Bear is a four-origin espresso blend anchored by the Philippines Sitio Belis estate, whose pulped natural processing brings vanilla, dark fruit, and stone fruit character alongside a smooth, chocolatey body from the Latin American washed components. The blend is roasted to medium specifically for espresso — expect a well-integrated cup that rewards you with caramelized sweetness and a clean finish rather than sharp edges.

- **Philippines component (pulped natural):** Stone fruit, vanilla, dark berry notes — the flavor highlight. The bloom phase gives this component time to degas and open up before extraction pressure builds.
- **Latin American components (washed):** Chocolate, caramel, structural backbone — keep the cup grounded and approachable.
- **Altitude (1400–1700m):** Moderate density; should dial in predictably, no extreme quirks.
- **Freshness:** Very fresh at purchase (3 days off roast). Give it 5–7 days before expecting the fruit notes to fully settle and integrate.

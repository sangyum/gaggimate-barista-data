## What to Expect

A Central American washed blend aimed at approachable daily drinking. Clean cup with a chocolate-caramel spine and a light citrus top note.

- **Origin character:** Costa Rica brings balance and clean sweetness; Guatemala adds chocolate body and mild cocoa. Together they produce a well-rounded, non-challenging profile.
- **Processing (washed):** Minimal fermentation character — the sweet lemon note is origin-driven acidity, not process-driven funk. Responds predictably to classic 9-bar extraction.
- **Use case:** Excellent milk-drink bean (the caramel/cocoa carries through) and decent straight if dialed slightly coarser for clarity on the citrus note.

## What to Expect

A traditional Italian commodity espresso blend engineered for consistency and broad palate appeal, not terroir expression. Without Robusta in the mix, the ceiling is higher than the Super Crema sibling — cleaner finish, more chocolate/nut clarity — but it remains a multi-origin commodity blend designed around classic 9 bar extraction.

- **Origin character:** Multi-origin Latin/South American Arabicas produce a chocolate-nut-caramel comfort profile with low-to-moderate acidity. Don't expect single-origin fruit or florals.
- **Roast level:** Medium roast at full body suggests development past first crack but well shy of second — sweet baseline, restrained acidity, good crema.
- **Packaging vs freshness:** Lavazza ships with degassing valves and engineers blends for 12+ month shelf life. 43 days off roast (today) is not a freshness penalty for this category.
- **Best use case:** Aerocano base (1:5 ratio holds up well to full-bodied shots), latte art practice, daily-driver shots. Won't compete with specialty single-origins pulled neat.

## What to Expect

MVP is Mostra's straightforward crowd-pleaser — a three-origin blend spotlighting Mexico, Vietnam, and Philippines producers, roasted to medium-dark for everyday drinkability. Expect a well-integrated cup with caramelized sweetness up front, a chocolatey mid-palate, and a gentle orange finish that keeps things interesting without getting bright or sharp.

- **Philippines component:** Familiar anchor — the same estate that makes the Philippines Heritage sing. Adds smooth sweetness and dark fruit undertones.
- **Vietnam component:** Typically adds body, earthiness, and weight — keeps the blend grounded and ensures a full, satisfying mouthfeel.
- **Mexico component:** Balanced and clean; rounds out the acidity and helps the orange note integrate rather than stand alone.
- **Medium-dark roast:** Well into the caramelization range — sugar sweetness is front and center. The orange reads as a zesty finish, not a bright acid note.
